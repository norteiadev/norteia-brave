<objective>
Close G1: Mtur destinos ingested from a single source have corroboracao_value=0, which caps the §7.6
score at 75.5 even after D-06 sets validacao_humana=100. With the 20% corroboracao weight, a
corroboracao_value=50 boost raises the score from 75.5 to 85.5 (≥85 threshold_mar), enabling Mar
promotion. This plan adds the IBGE corroboration boost step to the harness between ingest and steward
promotion, mirroring the pattern in brave/lanes/destinos/notebooklm.py (lines 214-221). It does NOT
change the global §7.6 gate, weights, or thresholds — the boost applies only in the harness and
stands in for the NotebookLM/2nd-source corroboration that would apply in a full production run.

Purpose: Make the load-test harness viable so destinos actually reach Mar. Without this, Step 2 always
produces 0 Mar promotions and Steps 3-4 produce no data.

Output: Updated scripts/loadtest_destinos_atrativos.py with corroboration step; new
tests/unit/test_harness_corroboration.py verifying the score math.
