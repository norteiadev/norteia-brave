<objective>
Add the operator-selectable pipeline **depth** to the engine state machine and its control endpoints. Depth is the cost-checkpoint contract for the whole phase: `nascente` (free) | `nascente_rio` (paid Places+LLM) | `nascente_rio_mar` (full + Mar push). This plan owns the persistence (Redis `brave:engine:depth`), the status read-back, and the server-side required-selection enforcement on `POST /start`.

Purpose: Make depth a first-class, persisted, server-validated engine attribute so the orchestrator (plan 10-02) and dashboard (plan 10-03) consume a fixed contract.
Output: Depth getter/setter + enum constants in `engine.py`, depth in `get_status`, validated `depth` body param on `/start`, and offline pytest coverage.
</objective>
