<objective>
Thread the engine **depth** (set in plan 10-01) through the orchestrator and the destinos lane so the three depths become real cost boundaries:
- `nascente`: Mtur-only seed → `store_raw` + §7.6 score, **no** `process_nascente_record` (Rio), **no** Desmembramento LLM, **no** atrativos discovery (Places). Zero external cost.
- `nascente_rio`: producers + Rio routing (mar-eligible/dlq/descarte), **no** atrativos WhatsApp-gate FSM kickoff.
- `nascente_rio_mar`: full pipeline as today.

Purpose: Turn the persisted depth into the actual gate on what gets dispatched and how far it flows, reading Redis depth ONCE in the orchestrator and passing it down as an explicit task arg (never scattered into the lanes).
Output: `depth` param on `engine_sweep_run` and `sweep_uf`, per-depth fan-out gating, a `run_rio` flag on `MturSeedIngest.produce`, and offline pytest coverage.
</objective>
