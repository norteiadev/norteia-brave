# Summary: Fix Celery worker registering zero tasks

## What changed
- `brave/tasks/celery_app.py` — `app.autodiscover_tasks(["brave.tasks"], related_name="pipeline")`
  (was defaulting to `related_name="tasks"` → imported non-existent `brave.tasks.tasks`).
  Added an explanatory comment.
- `tests/unit/test_celery_task_registration.py` (new, 4 parametrized cases) — drives
  `app.loader.import_default_modules()` and asserts the 4 production tasks register
  without `--include`.

## Verification
- Offline: `pytest tests/unit/test_celery_task_registration.py -q` → **4 passed**
  (no Redis/Postgres, RUN_REAL_EXTERNALS unset).
- Live: restarted the worker as `celery -A brave.tasks.celery_app:app worker`
  (no `--include`) → `[tasks]` now lists `brave.engine_sweep_run`, `brave.sweep_uf`,
  `brave.process_nascente`, `brave.push_mar`. Before the fix the list was empty.

## Impact
Real 24/7 deployment: the worker now registers and processes tasks under the documented
launch command. Previously a dispatched `engine_sweep_run` would never be consumed and
the engine would hang in `running`.
